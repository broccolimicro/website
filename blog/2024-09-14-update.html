Pre-alpha release of Loom

Ned Bingham
September 14, 2024

We are preparing for the alpha release of a toolset for asynchronous circuit design. Our previously named Haystack has been renamed to Loom. Loom now integrates the Floret flow as well, and we're in the process of tying the two flows together to allow for end-to-end synthesis. We have pivoted our full attention to Loom with the end goal of making the final chip design much easier. Tooling really makes all the difference. All of this comes with a website redesign putting that alpha release front and center.
