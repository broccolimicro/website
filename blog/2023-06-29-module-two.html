Introduction to Self Timed Circuits Module 2

Ned Bingham
June 29, 2023

Today marks the completion of the second module of our Introduction to Self Timed Circuits course. This module covers templated synthesis, a method for thinking about and implementing Quasi-Delay Insensitive logic by hand. The next module will cover formal synthesis using the Haystack toolset. All of the lectures have been recorded and made publicly available at https://broccolimicro.io/courses.py.
