Months of Progress

Ned Bingham
May 27, 2024

Today we have a few updates. Since our last post, we have developed an automated cell layout engine called Floret. It is targeted at Skywater's 130nm process technology node and produces mostly DRC clean layouts. Further, we have deployed an updated website that makes our whitepaper accessible and puts our EDA tooling work front and center. Finally, we have have prepared to offer our self-timed circuit course again this summer and posted it on our main page. Over the next few weeks, we will be preparing Haystack and Floret for further development, then we start diving into circuit design!
