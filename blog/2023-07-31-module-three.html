Introduction to Self Timed Circuits Module 3

Ned Bingham
July 31, 2023

We have just completed the third module of our Introduction to Self Timed Circuits course. This module covers formal synthesis, a potentially automatable method for implementing Quasi-Delay Insensitive logic. The next module will cover a variety of advanced topics that integrate everything from the first three modules. All of the lectures have been recorded and made publicly available at https://broccolimicro.io/courses.py.
