Introduction to Self Timed Circuits Module 1

Ned Bingham
June 8, 2023

We have just completed the first module of our Introduction to Self Timed Circuits course. This module covers fundamental concepts including pipeline structures, failure modes, timing assumptions, programmatic representations at different levels of abstraction, and data encoding. Each lecture includes a walkthrough, working with real circuits in the ACT language with Skywater's 130nm process technology node. All of the lectures have been recorded and made publicly available at https://broccolimicro.io/courses.py.
