Introduction to Self Timed Circuits

Ned Bingham
August 31, 2023

We have completed our Introduction to Self Timed Circuits course. There are 24 lectures split into 4 modules: Fundamentals, Templated Synthesis, Formal Synthesis, and Advanced Topics. Each lecture includes a walkthrough, working with real circuits in the ACT language with Skywater's 130nm process technology node. All of the lectures have been recorded and made publicly available in our Courses page.
